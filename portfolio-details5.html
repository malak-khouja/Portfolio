Calorie Counter Web App

The Calorie Counter Web App is designed to help users track their daily food intake and monitor their calorie consumption. This user-friendly application allows individuals to set personalized calorie goals and keep track of meals throughout the day, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.

Key Features:

  • Set Calorie Goals: Users can define their daily calorie goals based on their fitness or dietary objectives.
  • Meal Tracking: Easily add meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks, while tracking the calories consumed in each meal.
  • Calorie Calculation: The app automatically calculates the remaining calories for the day based on your entries and goal.
  • User-Friendly Interface: A simple, intuitive design ensures ease of use for users of all technical skill levels.
  • Responsive Design: The app is fully responsive, making it accessible on any device, including desktop and mobile.

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: No backend required for basic functionality (client-side only)
  • Additional Tools: JavaScript libraries for handling data (e.g., localStorage to store meal entries)

Project Impact: This app helps users maintain a balanced diet by tracking their calorie intake, ensuring they stay on target with their nutritional goals. It's perfect for individuals looking to adopt a healthier lifestyle or maintain their weight.
